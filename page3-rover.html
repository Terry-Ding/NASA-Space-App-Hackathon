Waste-Derived Components for Mars Rover

Systems: these can be built from the earth, not part of the waste recycle procedure

  • The rover can be built by ROS
  • Autonomous system
  • Odometry system -- GPS
  • Drive system
  • Navigation system
  • Visual feedback

Power System

Multi-Mission Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generator (MMRTG)

The rover can be powered by a Multi-Mission Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generator (MMRTG), which converts heat from the natural radioactive decay of plutonium-238 into electricity. This reliable and sustainable power source.

Components that can be built from the waste on Mars

Fabrics → soft materials, insulation, covers

  • dust covers to protect the components from the sand on Mars
  • thermal insulation layers to protect the electronic devices from cold
  • flexible cable harness sleeve

Food packaging → melt the waste and convert them into 3D printer filament

  • Advantage: Huge amount of materials and lightweight
  • We can make the arms and tools with 3D printing
  • Screws and other small components can be made from 3D printing
  • Structural elements → frames and panels
  • Chassis and body frame of the rover
  • Mounts and brackets for sensors, motors, or batteries
  • Suspension elements

Foam packaging → lightweight cores and insulation

  • Thermal insulation inside the rover body
  • Vibration dampening for electronic devices
  • Wheel padding

EVA waste → Flexible components

  • Shock absorber for landing or rover joint
  • Protective bumper around the rover
  • Flexible seal for dust protection
