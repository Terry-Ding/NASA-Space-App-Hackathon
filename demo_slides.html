A closed-loop recycling and manufacturing system that transforms Mars habitat waste into valuable manufacturing feedstock, reducing Earth dependency by 60-80% while enabling on-demand production of tools and components. Residual waste is blended with Martian regolith to create construction materials‚Äîachieving true zero-waste operations.
